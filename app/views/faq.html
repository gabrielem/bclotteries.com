FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

WHAT IS THE NONCE?
The Nonce number is the unique, unpredictable, 100% random number used by miners to generate a new valid Block of the Bitcoin Blockchain.
HOW BLOCKCHAIN DRAWINGS WORK?
At BlockChain Lotteries we do not run software internally to calculate your bet results: you are betting directly on the last digits of the Bitcoin Blockchain future Nonce numbers.
When you send bitcoin to one of our Bet Address to place a bet, in few minutes your transaction will get included in a Block by the Bitcoin Miners Network (this happen when your transaction shows "1 Confirmation"). At this moment we take the Block where your Bet Transaction is included and use its unique Nonce number last digit (Lottery 9) or last 2 digits (Lottery 99) or last 3 digits (Lottery 999) as your bet result.
If you guessed the right Nonce result BlockChain Lotteries will immediately payout the won amount to the same Bitcoin address we received the bet from.

    Valid Bets
    Bets are valid only if their amount is exactly the Ticket amount shown in the Bet Address pop-up. Any Bet Transaction with an amount lower or higher than the Ticket amount will be considered invalid.
    All Invalid Bets will NOT be displayed in the Bet section and will be returned to the sender’s address after the invalid Bet Transaction has reached 6 Confirmations from the Bitcoin Network.
    The Bitcoin Network Fee (currently 0.0002 Btc) will be deducted from the invalid bet amount. If the invalid bet amount is equal or less than 0.0002 Btc the transaction will be held.
    Transaction Fees
    Be sure to add network fees in your bet transaction!
    We strongly recommend players to send network fees sufficient to ensure all mining pool operators will accept the transaction (currently 0.0002 Btc per KB).
    Not including Network Fees to your Bet Transaction could result in late transaction processing by the Bitcoin Network. In simple words your Bet Transaction could not be included in the next available block, hence your Bet Result (that is based on the Nonce of the Block where your Bet Transaction is included) could be delayed by several hours.
    Multiple Bets
    Multiple Bets per transaction permitted!
    This could be a good way to save fees if you want to bet on different numbers on the same Block.
    Shared Wallets
    Shared wallets will not work correctly!
    Please only use wallets that allow you to receive Bitcoins on the same address you sent them from. This is not the case with Coinbase, Bitstamp, Btc-e wallets (or any kind of wallet where you do not control your Bitcoin keys).
